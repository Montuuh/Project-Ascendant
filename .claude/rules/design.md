# Design Document Rules

- design/ contains local design notes, system sketches, and ADRs.
- The GDD canonical source is Notion — do NOT duplicate GDD content here.
- design/ is for work-in-progress notes BEFORE they are written to Notion.
- Any design decision written here must be moved to Notion GDD and the
  local file either deleted or clearly marked as "Archived — see Notion §N.N.N"
- ADRs (Architecture Decision Records) live in design/adr/ and are permanent.
  Format: YYYY-MM-DD-decision-title.md
